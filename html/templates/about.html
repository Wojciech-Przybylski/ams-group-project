About QA Cinemas

Your Gateway to Classic and New Movies

QA Cinemas is an industry-recognised cinema chain with a rich history spanning over 20 years. Founded in 2004, we specialize in showcasing both the latest movie releases and special screenings of classic films from the 60's through the 90's.

With a strong presence in the Northwest of England, we are on a mission to expand our reach nationally while ensuring an enhanced online presence to connect with our audience.
