The UK film classification system (defined by The British Board of Film Classification) provides guidance to viewers about the content and suitability of movies. Here are the standard UK film classifications along with their appropriate icons:

Classification Icon Description
Universal (U) This film is suitable for all audiences, including children. It contains no content that would offend or harm any viewer.
Parental Guidance (PG) This film may contain mild content that requires parental guidance. Parents are urged to provide "parental guidance."
12A This film is suitable for children aged 12 and over, or younger children when accompanied by an adult.
12 Suitable only for persons of 12 years and over. Not to be supplied to any person below that age.
15 This film is suitable only for viewers aged 15 and over.
18 This film is suitable only for adults aged 18 and over. It may contain strong content such as violence, nudity, or strong language.
